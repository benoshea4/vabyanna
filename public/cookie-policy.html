Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work correctly and to provide information to website owners.

How We Use Cookies

This website (vabyanna.com) sets only strictly necessary cookies required for the site to function. We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or tracking cookies.

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are set in response to actions you take, such as setting your privacy preferences or filling in forms. They do not store any personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Cookies

If you use the Calendly booking link to schedule a consultation, Calendly may set its own cookies on their domain. We do not control these cookies. We recommend reviewing Calendly's privacy policy before booking:

  • Calendly Privacy Policy

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. For more information, visit aboutcookies.org. Disabling strictly necessary cookies may affect website functionality.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and the Irish ePrivacy Regulations, you have the right to refuse non-essential cookies. As this site only uses strictly necessary cookies, no consent banner is required. If you have any questions about how we handle cookies, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
